ACTIVATE LICENCE APPLICATION

You must accept the conditions of the Letter of Offer within 30 days otherwise your Preliminary Application will be automatically revoked.
Accepting the conditions of the Letter of Offer will activate your Sea Fishing Boat Licence Application. The application will be linked to your account and listed in My Applications (the ARN and PIN are no longer needed to retrieve the application).
You will then have one year to comply with the conditions of the Letter of Offer in order to be issued your licence.
